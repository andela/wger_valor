Features


Why
choose wger

Weight Tracker: Keep track of your progress with the body weight chart. This makes it easy to see if you are reaching your goals or need to adjust something.

Calendar: Get an overview of how and when you trained as well as how you personally felt you performed. Access all your individual diary entries with one click.

Exercise Database: The exercise database contains more than hundred exercises from which you can choose when creating a workout. You can view them by category and equipment used or muscles trained.

Gym Management: Use the application for your gym to manage its members, their workouts and attendance. You can manage the members' contracts, keep notes and upload any files you might need.

REST API: wger provides a REST API with full read and write access to all data so you can integrate your own application and tools.
